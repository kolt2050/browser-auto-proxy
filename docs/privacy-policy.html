Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Auto Proxy extension ("we", "our", or "the extension") handles information.

1. Data Collection

The Auto Proxy extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or personally identifiable information (PII) to any external servers.

2. Data Usage and Storage

All data used by the extension, including proxy configurations (IP, port, credentials) and custom site lists, is stored locally within your browser's isolated storage (chrome.storage.local and chrome.storage.sync). This data is used solely for the functionality of routing your web traffic as configured by you.

3. Third-Party Services

The extension may periodically download a geosite.dat file from public repositories (e.g., GitHub or JSDelivr) to update its domain database. These requests do not include any personal information about the user.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share any information with third parties, as no personal information is ever collected or stored on our servers.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
